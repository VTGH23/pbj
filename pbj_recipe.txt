1. Grab 2 slices of bread, peanut butter, and grape*** jelly
2. Using a knife, put peanut butter on one slice of bread
3. Using a knife, put grape*** jelly on the other slice of bread
4. Join the pieces of bread together so that the peanut butter and grape jelly are touching
5. Enjoy!

6. make sure to add extra bacon
***Please note that any flavor of jelly can be substituted for grape for a more enjoyable flavor
